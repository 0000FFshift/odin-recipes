Street Tacos

Description

Who doesn't love tacos? Especially traditional street tacos. Keep it simple with these chicken tacos served on heated corn tortillas and garnished with cilantro and onions.

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • 1lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 limes cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Heat tortillas
  3. Fill tortilla with chicken and garnish with onion cilantro and salsa
