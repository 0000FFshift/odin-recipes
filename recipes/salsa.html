Fire Roasted Salsa

Description

If you are going to eat tacos and nachos, it makes sense to make a batch of homemade salsa as well. It tastes so much better than any jarred salsa you can get in a grocery store and doesn't take a lot of effort.

Ingredients

  • 5 jalape√±os, stems removed
  • 3 serranos, stems removed
  • 1 habanero, stem removed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 5 roma tomatoes, cut in half
  • 1 bunch cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 1 white onion, sliced into rings
  • 2 limes, juiced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit
  2. To sheet pan, add all peppers, garlic, tomatoes, and sliced white onion
  3. Roast tomatoes and peppers until blackened. Once blackened, flip. When garlic starts to get charred, remove from pan.
  4. Once both sides of peppers and tomatoes are blackened, dump all peppers, garlic, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, and lime juice into food processor and pulse until salsa is at a consistency you like
  5. Season salsa with salt and more lime juice to taste
